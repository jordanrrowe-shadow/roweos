Install RoweOS as an App

For the best experience, add RoweOS to your device before continuing. If you skip this step, you may need to re-enter your setup later.

  1. Open roweos.com in Safari
  2. Click File then Add to Dock (or Share then Add to Dock)
  3. RoweOS will open as its own app window
  1. Open roweos.com in Chrome or Edge
  2. Click the install icon in the address bar, or Menu then "Install RoweOS"
  3. RoweOS will appear in your Start menu
  1. Open roweos.com in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (the square with an arrow pointing up)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. RoweOS will appear as an app on your home screen
  1. Open roweos.com in Chrome
  2. Tap Menu (three dots) then "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"
  3. RoweOS will appear as an app on your device

You can always install later from Settings, but we recommend doing it now to avoid re-entering your setup.
