Design Decisions Summary
Color: Coastal Blue (#edf2f8 to #2c4f6b) light mode, Midnight (#1e3a5f) dark mode
Naming: "Tax Intelligence" (display), tax-copilot (internal code key)
Typography: Cormorant Garamond serif headlines, DM Sans body
Category: Single "LifeAI Campaigns" sidebar entry, grouped internally by campaign
Templates: 17 designs x 2 modes = 34 total
Badges: LifeAI pill on every post, roweos.com watermark
Disclaimer: *AI-powered guidance built on IRS knowledge. Not tax, legal, or financial advice.
Coach accent colors preserved: Life Coach #22c55e, Wellness #06b6d4, Tax Intelligence #f59e0b, Personal AI #a78bfa, Standard AI #b2997b
