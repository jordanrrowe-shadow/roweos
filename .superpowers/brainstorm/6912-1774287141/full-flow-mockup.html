The Flow:

  1. Sidebar click → Split Panel landing page (info + navigation)
  2. Click a feature card → Enter sub-section with breadcrumb + pill nav
  3. Pill nav → Switch between sub-sections without going back
  4. Click "Social Hub" in breadcrumb → Return to landing page
  5. Primary pills have accent borders, secondary pills are dimmer
  6. Active pill has filled background, others are outlined
