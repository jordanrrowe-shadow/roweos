Which symbiote behavior feels right for B.L.A.K.E.?

A. Edge Crawler

Tendrils creep along the edges of the chat panel like Venom's symbiote clinging to surfaces. Reacts to messages - reaches toward new text, recoils on delete. Lives on the border between UI and void.

B. Ambient Symbiote

A dark organic mass that floats behind the chat, stretching tendrils and morphing shape continuously. Breathes when idle, surges when thinking, splits into multiple reaching arms when responding. Replaces the current blob.

C. Free Roamer

An autonomous creature that wanders the entire screen - slithering between UI elements, wrapping around cards, pooling in corners. Has its own AI-like movement patterns. Can be "called" to the chat area when B.L.A.K.E. is active.
