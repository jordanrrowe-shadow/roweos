## I. BRAND ESSENCE

RoweOS is a private AI-powered operating system designed for brand operators and individuals who demand more from their tools. It is not a chatbot. It is not a dashboard. It is a unified intelligence platform that thinks in terms of brands, coaches, workflows, and outcomes.

At its core, RoweOS serves two worlds through a single interface:

- **BrandAI Mode** -- Business management powered by specialized AI agents that embody your brand's identity, voice, and strategy
- **LifeAI Mode** -- Personal life management powered by coach archetypes that guide wellness, goals, finances, and daily operations

The platform runs as a single-file HTML application with zero dependencies on build tools, frameworks, or bundlers. All data lives in the user's browser (localStorage) with optional Firebase cloud sync. API keys are user-configured and never leave the device. This is software built for sovereignty -- your data, your keys, your intelligence.

### Core Philosophy

> "Quiet competence."

RoweOS does not hype. It does not sell. It operates. Every interaction reflects the philosophy that premium tools should feel like a trusted advisor -- confident, restrained, and precise. The platform draws from Apple-like design restraint: dark by default, glass morphism for depth, gold accents for identity, and SVG iconography over emoji without exception.

## VI. OS FUNCTIONALITY -- COMPLETE VIEW REFERENCE

### 1. Chat (Agent View)

The primary AI conversation interface. Supports full-context conversations with any BrandAI agent or LifeAI coach. Features include:

- **Dynamic system prompt construction** -- AI receives complete brand identity, knowledge, voice, audience, positioning, and constraints with every message
- **File attachment** -- Upload documents as context chips injected into the conversation
- **Model selection** -- Switch between Anthropic Claude (Opus 4.6, Sonnet 4.5, Haiku 4.5), OpenAI GPT-4o, and Google Gemini 3.0 models mid-conversation
- **Conversation save/load** -- Save conversations to Library, resume later
- **Bubble vs. inline display** -- Toggle between chat-bubble and linear conversation views
- **Web search integration** -- Claude Web Search and Google Search Grounding for real-time information

### 2. Signal (Dashboard)

The Focus 2.0 intelligence dashboard. A customizable widget grid providing at-a-glance operational awareness:

- **Mini Calendar Widget** -- Today's date with month/year navigation, vertical and horizontal orientations
- **Day Detail Widget** -- Events, tasks, and automations for selected day
- **Automations Widget** -- Scheduled workflow preview and status

- **Drag-reorderable layout** -- Widgets can be repositioned and resized (compact, wide, full)
- **Category cards** -- Color-coded brand operation categories
- **Auto-save** -- Widget positions persist via localStorage

## IX. COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGES

### 1. Sovereign AI Architecture
Unlike cloud-dependent platforms, RoweOS runs entirely in the browser. API keys never leave the device. Data lives in localStorage or user-configured Firebase. No intermediary servers, no data harvesting, no vendor lock-in. Users bring their own AI provider keys (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google) and maintain complete ownership.

### 2. Multi-Model Intelligence
RoweOS is model-agnostic. Users can select from Claude Opus 4.6, Claude Sonnet 4.5, Claude Haiku 4.5, GPT-4o, Gemini 3.0 Pro, and Gemini 3.0 Flash -- switching mid-conversation or per operation. No other brand management platform offers this flexibility.

### 3. Brand-Embodied AI Agents
Agents do not just know about the brand -- they ARE the brand. Every response is generated through dynamically constructed system prompts that inject complete brand identity: essence, voice, tone, vocabulary (do/don't), audience, positioning, constraints, products, pricing, and uploaded knowledge documents. This goes far beyond simple "tone of voice" settings.

### 4. Dual-Mode Operation (BrandAI + LifeAI)
No competitor offers both business brand management and personal life coaching in one platform. The cross-mode intelligence sharing (Tax Copilot receiving business data, Life Coach receiving brand context) creates a uniquely holistic operator experience.

### 5. Evidence-First Tax Copilot
The Tax Copilot is not a generic financial assistant. It operates with IRS publication-level compliance guardrails: no invented expenses, reconciliation of all income, citation of rules for every recommendation, and audit-ready documentation output. This is professional-grade tax preparation guidance embedded in a personal AI platform.

### 6. 150+ Pre-Built Operations
Every brand in the portfolio has purpose-built Studio operations. A dog training business gets intake forms and handler manuals. A luxury rental gets property listing optimizers and guest message sequences. A concierge service gets membership tier guides and vendor partnership briefs. These are not generic templates -- they are industry-specific, brand-aware workflows.

### 7. Deep Research Capability
The Research Agent connects to Gemini's Interactions API for 5-30 minute intensive research sessions. Market research reports, competitive landscapes, industry trends, regulatory reviews -- all grounded in real-time web data, synthesized through the brand's strategic context.

### 8. Zero-Framework Architecture
104,208 lines of pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript in a single file. No React, no Vue, no build step, no node_modules. This means instant deployment (one file to Vercel), zero dependency vulnerabilities, and complete transparency -- every line of code is readable and auditable.

### 9. Full PWA Support
RoweOS installs as a native-feeling app on iOS and Android. Standalone display mode, custom splash screens, offline capability, home screen installation. The platform works without internet, queuing changes until reconnected.

### 10. Cross-Device Firebase Sync
Optional real-time sync across devices for 15 data categories. Selective sync (choose cloud vs. local per category), automatic background sync every 60 seconds, force push/pull controls, and offline queue with automatic reconnection. Secured with Firestore per-user isolation rules.
